About The university

The high Tech University is the university with the longest tradition. Founded in 2021, it offers excellent teaching in a wide range of subjects in a total of 10 departments to its over 22.000 students. Trendsetting topics with high relevance for society determine research in Shanghai.

Our Campus

Strategically located in a beautiful residential district of Shanghai, our campus—an accredited arboretum—combines the advantages of a traditional college setting with the energy, culture, and opportunities of a global capital city.

Our Leaders

High tech University is led by Kawthar Makki, our first female president. The University's governance is directed by the Board of Trustees and senior university administrators.

Working@HTU

At High Tech University, we share a passion for creating change through our knowledge and our work. Explore job opportunities with us and learn how you can become a part of our community.
